Manage Sections

Instructions:
  • Click Add Section to create a new section for your course. You can add a Title, Video (YouTube or upload), and Notes (PDF or text).
  • Use View All Sections to see, edit, or remove your existing sections.
  • Organize your sections to provide a smooth learning experience for your students.
  • Keep section titles clear and concise for easy navigation.
  • Upload high-quality videos and notes to enhance understanding.
  • Update your sections regularly based on student feedback and course updates.
